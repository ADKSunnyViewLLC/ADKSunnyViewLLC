The future development of Sunny View Park will bring housing and community to the north country with places real people can afford.

Vision Overview

Sunny View Park is planned to include 16 manufactured home sites and a central community area, combining affordability, comfort, and a sense of neighborhood.

Planned Features

Affordable Housing

Modern double-wide homes designed for efficiency and durability.

Community Center

A shared space for gatherings, recreation, and neighborhood events.

Green Spaces

Open areas, walking paths, and tree preservation for a natural feel.
