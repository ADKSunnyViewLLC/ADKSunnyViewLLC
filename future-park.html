Future Park Expansion

The next phase of ADK SunnyView LLC focuses on disciplined expansion across the rear portion of the property. This future mobile home park is designed to complement the existing community while increasing housing capacity, long-term revenue stability, and overall land value.

Conceptual visualization representing evenly spaced mobile home plots arranged across the back half of the property, surrounded by preserved woodland and internal access roads.

Planned Capacity

Up to sixteen (16) additional mobile home plots are planned for the expansion phase, maintaining balanced density and consistent spacing throughout the park.

Phased Construction

Development will proceed in controlled phases, allowing infrastructure to scale efficiently while minimizing disruption to existing residents and surrounding land.

Infrastructure Extension

Roads, utilities, and service corridors will extend naturally from the existing development, leveraging prior investments and reducing overall project cost.

Environmental Integration

The future park layout emphasizes harmony with the natural environment. Tree buffers, open sightlines, and green corridors are incorporated to preserve the wooded character of the land while supporting residential use.

Long-Term Value Creation

By expanding deliberately and maintaining consistent standards, ADK SunnyView LLC positions the property as a long-term income-producing asset with resilient demand and strong operational fundamentals.
