Future Manufactured Home Park

The next phase of ADK SunnyView LLC is a thoughtfully designed manufactured home community built on elevated ground at the rear of the property — creating separation, resilience, and long-term value.

This development is intentionally positioned to more than double the overall value of the land while strengthening the surrounding community through stability, pride of ownership, and shared space.

Elevated Design, Elevated Value

The existing single-wide homes remain along the main road at a lower elevation, preserving access and continuity for long-term residents.

The new manufactured home park will rise approximately 20 feet higher on the rear acreage, offering improved drainage, views, privacy, and a distinct identity as a next-generation residential community.

Elevation is not just physical — it represents an intentional step forward in quality, planning, and asset performance. Double-wide homes placed on higher ground reflect permanence, care, and future readiness.

Conceptual Rear Park Layout

The rear 10 acres will be organized into evenly spaced double-wide home sites surrounded by preserved forest, internal roadways, and pedestrian paths — all oriented toward a shared community center.

Community-Centered Growth

At the heart of the future park is a modern community center designed to serve residents of both the existing and new developments.

This shared space will host gatherings, services, meetings, and everyday connection — reinforcing the idea that growth should benefit everyone, not displace anyone.

Built for Generations

ADK SunnyView LLC approaches development with a long horizon. This manufactured home park is not a short-term build — it is a generational asset designed to provide housing, dignity, and value for decades to come.
