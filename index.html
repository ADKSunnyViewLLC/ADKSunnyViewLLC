Manufactured Home Communities

ADK SunnyView LLC is transforming an existing mobile home property into a long-term, community-centered manufactured housing development. Our strategy prioritizes quality construction, thoughtful land use, and durable value creation.

Development Strategy

The SunnyView property is being repositioned through a phased expansion that introduces modern double-wide manufactured homes on elevated land, while preserving and stabilizing the existing community along the roadway.

Existing Community

Sixteen single-wide homes remain in place along the main road, providing stable occupancy and immediate cash flow.

New Manufactured Park

A new double-wide manufactured home park will be developed on the elevated rear acreage, designed for long-term residency and higher asset value.

Value Expansion

The combination of elevation, unit quality, and community amenities is intended to significantly increase total property value over time.

Community-First Design

SunnyView is designed as a neighborhood, not a transient park. Planned amenities include a modern community center, shared green space, and layouts that balance privacy with connection.
