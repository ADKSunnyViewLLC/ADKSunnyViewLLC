Our Mission

ADK Sunny View LLC is dedicated to creating a thoughtful, sustainable manufactured home community in Peru, NY. Our focus is on quality homes, land stewardship, and a strong community vision that balances comfort, privacy, and environmental responsibility.

Core Principles

Responsible Development

Each phase of construction prioritizes long-term durability, safety, and environmental awareness.

Community Focus

Thoughtful layouts, shared amenities, and natural spaces foster a welcoming and cohesive neighborhood.

Transparency & Compliance

Clear documentation, trust structures, and site plans ensure transparency for both regulators and stakeholders.

Vision for the Future

We aim to blend modern manufactured homes with the beauty of the Adirondacks, creating a space that residents are proud to call home while preserving the surrounding natural landscape.
