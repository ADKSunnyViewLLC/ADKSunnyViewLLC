Development Strategy & Phasing

ADK Sunny View LLC plans the development of Sunny View Park in structured phases to ensure affordability, quality construction, and long-term value creation.

Construction Phases

Phase 1: Infrastructure

Road grading, utility connections, and lot preparation ensure a solid foundation for homes and community facilities.

Phase 2: Home Placement

Placement of manufactured homes on prepared lots with attention to spacing, privacy, and landscaping.

Phase 3: Community Facilities

Construction of the community center, common areas, and green spaces to foster resident engagement.

Phase 4: Finishing Touches

Landscaping, exterior finishes, and final inspections to ensure a polished, professional community.

Long-Term Value

By developing with care, the park maintains property value, environmental balance, and a strong sense of community for years to come.
