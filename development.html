Current Development

The SunnyView property currently operates as a stabilized mobile home park with existing infrastructure, roadway access, and long-term residents. Development to date has prioritized orderly placement, utility reliability, and preservation of the surrounding forested environment.

Property Overview

Approximately 20 acres of wooded land with a square-shaped layout. Existing mobile homes are positioned along the front third of the property, closest to road access and utilities.

Existing Units

Sixteen (16) mobile homes currently occupy the developed section of the park. Spacing and orientation allow for privacy, access, and future infrastructure extensions.

Infrastructure

Established road entry, utility connections, and service corridors form the backbone of the current layout, enabling scalable expansion without disruption to existing residents.

Land Use Philosophy

ADK SunnyView LLC develops land with restraint and foresight. By limiting density and preserving natural buffers, the current development maintains a quiet residential character while protecting long-term property value.

Prepared for Expansion

The rear two-thirds of the property remains undeveloped forest, intentionally reserved for future phases. This separation allows existing residents to live undisturbed while new development is introduced methodically.
