Current Development

ADK SunnyView LLC operates a stabilized manufactured home park while executing a long-term expansion strategy designed to significantly increase property value. Existing single-wide homes line the roadway at a lower elevation, while a new double-wide manufactured home community is planned on elevated land in the rear of the property.

Doubling Property Value Through Smart Expansion

The business plan centers on transforming underutilized rear acreage into a premium manufactured housing community. By elevating the new park approximately 20 feet above the existing homes and introducing larger double-wide residences, SunnyView creates a clear hierarchy of development, improved views, and long-term asset appreciation.

Development Logic

Locating the new manufactured homes on higher ground preserves privacy for existing residents while establishing a premium residential zone. This elevation strategy improves drainage, views, and market perception—key drivers in the property's projected value increase.
