Existing Park & Strategic Elevation Plan

ADK SunnyView LLC currently operates a stabilized single-wide mobile home park positioned along the main road frontage of the property.

This existing development forms the foundation of a broader strategy: preserving current value while unlocking substantial upside through intentional elevation and expansion.

Front Park — Established Single-Wide Homes

The existing park consists of single-wide homes located on the lower portion of the property, closest to the main road and original utility corridors.

Long-term residents benefit from established access, continuity, and stability while remaining insulated from future construction activity.

This section of the property remains intentionally unchanged — serving as the stable income base while new value is built behind it.

Rear Elevation — Future Manufactured Home Park

The rear acreage rises approximately 20 feet above the existing park, creating a natural separation that supports higher-quality development.

Value Repositioning Strategy

By introducing a new double-wide manufactured home park on elevated land, ADK SunnyView LLC is repositioning the property as a mixed-generation, multi-tier residential community.

This strategy is designed to more than double the total asset value of the property while enhancing livability, resilience, and long-term demand.
