Inside the Homes

The new double-wide manufactured homes at ADK SunnyView are designed with the same care you’d expect if the owners were your own parents—clean lines, warm materials, and layouts that support real family living.

Open, Livable Spaces

Living areas are designed to feel open and welcoming, with natural light, practical flow, and room to gather. These are spaces meant for everyday life— not temporary living.

Durable finishes and neutral palettes ensure longevity, comfort, and ease of maintenance for residents and ownership alike.

Kitchen & Dining

Kitchens are laid out for real use—meals, conversations, and daily routines. Storage, counter space, and appliance placement are all designed for efficiency without sacrificing warmth.

These interiors reflect long-term thinking: easy to maintain, timeless in appearance, and built to last.

Restful Bedrooms

Bedrooms are designed as quiet, private spaces—well-proportioned, calm, and comfortable. The goal is rest, stability, and a sense of home.

These homes are meant to be lived in for years, not months.
