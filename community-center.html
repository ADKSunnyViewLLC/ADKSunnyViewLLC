Community Center

The ADK SunnyView Community Center is designed as the heart of the property — a welcoming space where families gather, neighbors connect, and community life thrives across generations.

Exterior Concept — Elevated Gathering Hall

Positioned between the existing single-wide community and the future elevated manufactured home park, the Community Center serves as a visual and social anchor. Its 20×30 footprint and raised design complement the natural terrain while offering year-round accessibility.

Conceptual exterior inspired by modern Adirondack architecture — elevated, timber-framed, and designed to blend naturally with the surrounding landscape.

Interior Concept — Warm, Lodge-Style Space

Inside, the Community Center offers a warm, open environment with timber finishes, vaulted ceilings, and flexible seating. The space supports resident meetings, celebrations, educational programs, and quiet daily use.

Interior concept inspired by classic lodge design — open, welcoming, and designed for both large gatherings and intimate community moments.

Built for Community & Long-Term Value

More than an amenity, the Community Center reinforces ADK SunnyView’s commitment to responsible development. By investing in shared spaces, the property supports resident well-being, strengthens neighborhood ties, and contributes to long-term property value growth.
