Community Center

The SunnyView Community Center is a planned shared facility positioned between the existing mobile home park and the future expansion area. It is designed to anchor community life while supporting long-term resident stability.

The center serves as connective infrastructure — not just a building, but a stabilizing asset for the entire property.

Shared Space

Flexible interior design allows for meetings, events, and resident services without overbuilding or unnecessary complexity.

Strategic Placement

Located between existing and future development phases, the center creates cohesion while minimizing disruption.

Long-Term Value

Community infrastructure improves retention, reduces turnover, and enhances overall asset durability.

Built for Residents

The community center prioritizes practical use: administrative functions, resident meetings, shared resources, and social gatherings.

Aligned With Growth

As SunnyView expands, the center scales naturally with demand, supporting increased population without sacrificing order or quality.
