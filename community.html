Community Center – The Heart of Sunny View Park

The community center is essential for creating a close-knit, supportive neighborhood. It provides residents with a welcoming space for events, recreation, meetings, and shared activities, helping to strengthen bonds and foster a sense of belonging within Sunny View Park.

Community Center Features

Multipurpose Rooms

Spaces designed for meetings, classes, and social gatherings.

Recreation & Fitness

Indoor areas for exercise, games, and group activities.

Event Hosting

Large gathering spaces for community celebrations and events.

Shared Lounge Areas

Comfortable common areas where residents can connect and relax.
